RaftingTours360

Your ultimate destination for unforgettable rafting experiences.

RaftingTours360 is a trip agency focused on providing the best experience in rafting tours.

Our History

Founded in 2010 by Alex Carter and Mia Rivera, RaftingTours360 began as a small operation near the Colorado River, offering guided tours with a commitment to safety and eco-friendly practices. Over the years, we have expanded to include multi-day expeditions, incorporating camping, hiking, and digital tools to enhance the adventure.

Today, we offer tours across North America for all skill levels, from beginner-friendly floats to adrenaline-pumping rapids, inspiring a love for nature and the thrill of whitewater rafting.
